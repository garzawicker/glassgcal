Google Calendar for Google Glass

Readme

Version 1.0

1. Just hit send to calendar to send a bundled item to your timeline. You can pin it to the left of your home screen for easy access.
2. Click on the bundle to view details of your upcoming 10 events, or just view the ones that fit on the main screen.
3. The cards should refresh every hour from the time you added it, or you can manually refresh the card.

Currently it only pulls from your primary calendar, whatever you have set to primary. I do not feel the need to expand to others as this would defeat the purpose of quick information from Glass.
